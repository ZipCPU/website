Gisselquist Technology, LLC

Gisselquist Technology, LLC, is a service disabled, veteran owned, small business focused on offering services specializing in both embedded and FPGA solutions as well as digital signal processing solutions. We have but one employee, Dan Gisselquist (that’s me!). I have a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from the U.S. Air Force Institute of Technology, a Master’s of Science in Computer Engineering from the same, and two Bachelor of Science degrees, one in Mathematics and the other in Computer Science, from the U.S. Air Force Academy. While I am not the greatest guru out there, I do take a particular joy in solving problems that others cannot solve, and I like to think of this as Gisselquist Technology’s niche.

Gisselquist Technology is founded upon a couple of values:

  • We are a Bible believing, Christian based company.

    o We believe that Jesus is the Christ, and follow his teachings

    o We are closed on Sundays

    o We are committed to the truth (Matt 5:37). This includes not only the truth of God’s creation (Gen 1:27, Matt 19:4), but also the truth of how our performance compares against other systems when known. It also means we don’t hide our faults.

    o I’d love to discuss the Bible with you, and the Gospel Jesus delivered

  • Debt free. Please don’t offer us a business loan, I get enough annoying phone calls as it is.

The ZipCPU blog, featuring how to discussions of FPGA and soft-core CPU design. This site will be focused on Verilog solutions, using exclusively OpenSource IP products for FPGA design. Particular focus areas include topics often left out of more mainstream FPGA design courses such as how to debug an FPGA design.
